# V2X Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-25

V2X is an open-source V2EX client. This policy explains what data is processed when you use the iOS app.

## Data You Provide

- When you sign in, browse member-only pages, check in, create topics, create replies, favorite content, ignore content, or read notifications, the app sends the necessary requests to V2EX to perform those actions.
- Topic drafts, reply drafts, account display information, app settings, search history, proxy settings, and check-in settings may be stored locally on your device.
- Search keywords are sent to the search service used by the app to return V2EX search results.

## Photos

V2X requests add-only photo library access only when you save an image from the app to your system photo library. The app does not read your photo library.

## Notifications And Background Refresh

If enabled, V2X may use notifications and background refresh to support automatic check-in and related reminders. You can change these permissions in iOS Settings.

## Analytics And Crash Reports

The iOS app includes Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics. These services are used for basic usage statistics, crash diagnostics, and stability improvements. They are not used for advertising tracking.

## Tracking

V2X does not use App Tracking Transparency tracking, does not sell personal data, and does not share app data with data brokers.

## Third-Party Services

V2X communicates with:

- V2EX, for forum browsing and account features.
- The search service used by this project, for search results.
- Firebase, for analytics and crash diagnostics.

Each third-party service may process data according to its own privacy practices.

## Contact

For privacy questions or support requests, use the support options listed on the V2X support page.
